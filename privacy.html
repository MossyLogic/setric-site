Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Setric is a workout tracking app for iOS. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

The short version: Setric collects no personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data We Collect

Setric does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data you enter — workouts, exercises, sessions, preferences — is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's AsyncStorage and never leaves your device.

We do not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or any third-party data collection services.

Data Storage

All workout data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all data associated with it will be permanently deleted. We have no way to recover it.

Third Party Services

Setric does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal data. The app does not require an account or internet connection to function.

Children's Privacy

Setric is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@setric.app.
